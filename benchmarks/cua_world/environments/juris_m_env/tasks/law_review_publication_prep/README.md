# law_review_publication_prep

## Overview
A law review editor must prepare a Juris-M library of 15 items (8 law review articles + 7 court cases) for a special issue on Technology and Privacy Law. The library is currently unorganized with no collections, missing abstracts on some articles, and no tags.

## Goal
1. Create collection hierarchy: parent 'Tech & Privacy Special Issue' with subcollections 'Data Privacy Articles', 'Digital Search Cases', 'Surveillance Law'
2. Assign all 15 items to the appropriate subcollection
3. Add abstracts to the 4 articles missing them
4. Create a standalone 'Table of Contents' note listing all 15 items
5. Set Quick Copy citation style to Chicago Manual of Style 17th edition (full note)
6. Tag all 15 items with 'tech-privacy-2024'

## Scoring
- Collection hierarchy (parent + 3 subcollections): 20 pts
- All 15 items assigned to subcollections: 20 pts
- 4 missing abstracts added: 15 pts
- Table of Contents note exists (standalone, >=200 chars): 15 pts
- Chicago citation style set: 10 pts
- 'tech-privacy-2024' tag on >=12 items: 10 pts
- Classification roughly correct (articles not in Digital Search Cases): 10 pts
Pass threshold: 60 points
