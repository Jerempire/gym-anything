  - Different case ("john" vs "John")

  - Different encoding

  Changes to export_result.sh:

  1. Case-insensitive matching with LOWER() and TRIM():
  WHERE LOWER(TRIM(fname))='john' AND LOWER(TRIM(lname))='testpatient'
