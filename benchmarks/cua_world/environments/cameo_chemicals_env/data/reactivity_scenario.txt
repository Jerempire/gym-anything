CHEMICAL MIXING HAZARD ASSESSMENT REQUEST
==========================================
Date: 2025-11-20
Facility: Meridian Chemical Manufacturing Plant
Location: Building 7, Mixing and Storage Area
Priority: URGENT — Forklift Damage Incident

INCIDENT CONTEXT:
At approximately 09:45 this morning, a forklift operator in Building 7
damaged a 25-kg container of Sodium Cyanide in Storage Bay C-1 while
repositioning pallets. The damaged container is approximately 15 meters
from the Sulfuric Acid storage tanks in Bay B-2. Although no spill has
occurred yet, the container integrity is compromised and the plant
safety team requires an immediate chemical mixing hazard assessment.

CHEMICALS INVOLVED:
  Chemical 1:
    Name:        Sulfuric Acid (concentrated, 98%)
    UN Number:   1830
    CAS Number:  7664-93-9
    Location:    Building 7, Bay B-2
    Quantity:    750 kg in polyethylene tank

  Chemical 2:
    Name:        Sodium Cyanide (solid)
    UN Number:   1689
    CAS Number:  143-33-9
    Location:    Building 7, Bay C-1 (container damaged)
    Quantity:    25 kg in sealed container (compromised)

PRIMARY CONCERN:
The safety team suspects that mixing sulfuric acid with sodium cyanide
could generate Hydrogen Cyanide (HCN) gas, which is extremely toxic
(IDLH: 50 ppm). We need the CAMEO Chemicals reactivity prediction
tool to confirm this hazard and identify ALL predicted reaction
products and associated risks.

ASSESSMENT REQUIRED:
  1. Navigate to CAMEO Chemicals reactivity prediction tool
  2. Add Sulfuric Acid and Sodium Cyanide to the prediction
  3. Document ALL predicted hazards:
     - Toxic gas generation (specifically HCN)
     - Heat generation
     - Fire/explosion risk
     - Other reaction products
  4. Note severity ratings for each hazard
  5. Provide emergency response recommendations based on findings

OUTPUT FILE:
  ~/Documents/reactivity_report.txt

FORMAT:
  Include chemical names, predicted reactions, hazard descriptions,
  severity levels, and recommended actions.
