CHEMICAL UNDER REVIEW:

  CAS Number: 71-43-2
  UN Number:  1114
  OSHA PEL:   1 ppm (TWA), 5 ppm (STEL)
  ACGIH TLV:  0.5 ppm (TWA), 2.5 ppm (STEL)

PURPOSE:
Compile comprehensive physical and chemical property data for benzene
as part of the annual workplace hazard assessment per OSHA 29 CFR
1910.1028 (Benzene Standard). This data will be used to update the
facility Safety Data Sheet (SDS) cross-reference file and to evaluate
current engineering controls for the benzene distillation column.

REQUIRED PROPERTIES (from CAMEO Chemicals datasheet):
  1.  Boiling Point
  2.  Melting Point / Freezing Point
  3.  Flash Point
  4.  Vapor Pressure (at 20°C or 25°C)
  5.  Specific Gravity (relative to water)
  6.  Lower Explosive Limit (LEL / LFL)
  7.  Upper Explosive Limit (UEL / UFL)
  8.  IDLH (Immediately Dangerous to Life or Health)
  9.  Vapor Density (relative to air)
  10. Water Solubility

OUTPUT FILE:
  ~/Documents/benzene_properties.txt

FORMAT:
  Each property on its own line with name, value, and units.
  Example:
    Boiling Point: 80.1 °C (176.2 °F)
