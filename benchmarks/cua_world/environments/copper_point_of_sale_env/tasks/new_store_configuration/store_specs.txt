NEW STORE SETUP SPECIFICATIONS
Meridian Goods & Supply — Cleveland Location
================================================

Complete ALL configuration steps below in Copper Point of Sale.

================================================
SECTION 1: BUSINESS INFORMATION
Set the following in Options/Settings > Business Info (or similar):

  Business Name:  Meridian Goods & Supply
  Address Line 1: 4720 Commerce Parkway, Suite 100
  City/State/ZIP:  Cleveland, OH 44135
  Phone:           (216) 555-0294
  Website:         www.meridiangoodssupply.com

================================================
SECTION 2: RECEIPT CONFIGURATION
  Header Message:  Premium Quality, Fair Prices
  Footer Message:  Thank you for shopping Meridian Goods! Returns accepted
                   within 21 days with receipt. meridiangoodssupply.com
  Enable: Print date and time on receipts
  Enable: Print receipt footer

================================================
SECTION 3: TAX SETTINGS
  Default Tax Rate: 8.00%
  (This applies to all taxable items unless overridden per item/category)

================================================
SECTION 4: INVENTORY CATEGORIES
Add these 4 custom categories with the specified tax rates:

  Category Name         Tax Rate
  ----------------------------------
  Food & Grocery        0.00% (tax exempt)
  Electronics & Tech    9.50%
  Apparel               8.00%
  Gift Cards            0.00% (tax exempt)

================================================
SECTION 5: PAYMENT METHODS
Verify or add the following payment methods are available:
  - Cash (should already exist)
  - Credit Card (should already exist)
  - Check  ← Add this if not present

================================================
SECTION 6: VERIFICATION TEST
After completing all configuration above:
  1. Add one inventory item: "Varsity Top Test" in the Apparel category,
     price $60.00, taxable at 8.00%
  2. Process a $60.00 cash sale of this item
  3. Note the tax amount shown during checkout
  4. Save your verification to: C:\Users\Docker\Desktop\tax_verification.txt

  Format for tax_verification.txt:
  Item: Varsity Top Test
  Price: $60.00
  Tax Rate: 8.00%
  Tax Amount: $X.XX
  Total: $XX.XX

================================================
