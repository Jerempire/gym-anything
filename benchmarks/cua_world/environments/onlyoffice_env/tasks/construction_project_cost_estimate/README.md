# Construction Project Cost Estimate

## Task Overview

Prepare a detailed construction cost estimate for a 4,200 square foot medical office build-out at 550 Commerce Drive for Lakewood Medical Partners. The agent must synthesize project specifications, current material pricing, and prevailing labor rates into a professional cost estimate workbook with line items, trade coverage, formulas, subtotals, markups, and a grand total.

## Occupation / Industry

Construction Estimating / Commercial Construction

## Difficulty

very_hard

## Source Data Description

- **Project specifications**: `~/Documents/project_specifications.txt` -- detailed build-out requirements for a 4,200 SF medical office
- **Material pricing**: `~/Documents/Spreadsheets/material_prices.csv` -- current unit costs for construction materials
- **Labor rates**: `~/Documents/Spreadsheets/labor_rates.csv` -- prevailing wage rates by trade
- **Expected cost range**: $400,000 - $750,000 grand total (including overhead, profit, contingency, and permits)

## Expected Outcome

A professional cost estimate workbook saved as `~/Documents/Spreadsheets/medical_office_estimate.xlsx` containing:
- Detailed line items with quantities, unit costs, and extensions
- Coverage of major CSI divisions and trades (concrete, metal, wood, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, etc.)
- Formulas for line item calculations (qty x unit cost)
- Subtotals organized by division or trade
- Markup calculations (overhead ~10%, profit ~8%, contingency ~5%)
- Grand total in the plausible range of $400K-$750K

## Verification Criteria

| Criterion | Points | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Line items with quantities and costs | 2.0 | 15+ key construction items with quantity/unit/cost columns present |
| Coverage of CSI divisions | 2.0 | 8+ of 15 expected trade divisions represented (concrete, HVAC, electrical, etc.) |
| Formulas present | 1.5 | 10+ formulas for line item extensions and calculations |
| Subtotals by division | 1.0 | Division or trade subtotals present with intermediate sums |
| Markup calculations | 2.0 | Overhead, profit, and contingency line items with percentage-based calculations |
| Grand total in plausible range | 1.5 | Final total between $400,000 and $750,000 |

**Total: 10.0 points**

## Success Threshold

A score of 5.0 / 10.0 or higher (normalized to 0.50) is required to pass.
