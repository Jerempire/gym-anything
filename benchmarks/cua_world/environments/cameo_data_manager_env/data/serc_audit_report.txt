VERMONT STATE EMERGENCY RESPONSE COMMISSION (SERC)
TIER II DATA AUDIT FINDINGS

Facility:      Lakeside Chemical Supply
Address:       330 Reservoir Road, Montpelier, VT 05602
Audit Date:    January 8, 2025
Reference:     SERC-2025-VT-AUDIT-0047
Auditor:       Vermont SERC Data Quality Unit

================================================================
SUMMARY
================================================================
A physical site inspection and records review was conducted at
Lakeside Chemical Supply on January 8, 2025. The inspection
identified four discrepancies between the facility's submitted
Tier II data (as stored in CAMEO) and actual facility conditions.
Each finding requires corrective action in the CAMEO database.

================================================================
FINDING #1 — CHEMICAL QUANTITY DISCREPANCY
================================================================
Chemical:        Hydrogen Peroxide (CAS 7722-84-1)
Discrepancy:     The Tier II record shows an average daily amount
                 of 8,500 lbs. However, physical inspection of the
                 storage tanks confirms a maximum single-tank
                 capacity of 4,200 lbs. The reported average
                 exceeds the physically possible on-site quantity.
Corrective Action: Update the average daily amount for Hydrogen
                   Peroxide to 4,200 lbs in CAMEO.

================================================================
FINDING #2 — INCORRECT STORAGE LOCATION
================================================================
Chemical:        Sulfuric Acid (CAS 7664-93-9)
Discrepancy:     The Tier II record lists the storage location as
                 "Chemical Storage A". Physical inspection confirmed
                 that all sulfuric acid drums are stored in a
                 separate secondary containment area designated as
                 "Drum Storage Building B". The current CAMEO record
                 does not match the actual storage location.
Corrective Action: Update the storage location description for
                   Sulfuric Acid from "Chemical Storage A" to
                   "Drum Storage Building B" in CAMEO.

================================================================
FINDING #3 — INVALID EMERGENCY CONTACT INFORMATION
================================================================
Contact:         David Nguyen (Fac. Emergency Coordinator)
Discrepancy:     The listed emergency contact phone number for
                 David Nguyen (802-555-0177) has been verified as
                 disconnected. Mr. Nguyen is no longer employed at
                 this facility. His replacement, Patricia Okonkwo,
                 has taken over the Emergency Response Coordinator
                 role.
Replacement Contact:
  Name:          Patricia Okonkwo
  Title:         Emergency Response Coordinator
  Phone:         802-555-0293
  Contact Types: Fac. Emergency Coordinator, Emergency Contact
Corrective Action: Remove or update David Nguyen's contact record.
                   Add Patricia Okonkwo with the information above
                   as the new Fac. Emergency Coordinator and
                   Emergency Contact in CAMEO.

================================================================
FINDING #4 — INCORRECT FIRE DISTRICT ASSIGNMENT
================================================================
Discrepancy:     The facility record lists fire district as
                 "Station 4 - Montpelier South". However, based on
                 the facility's address at 330 Reservoir Road,
                 the correct responding fire station is Station 12,
                 which serves the northern reservoir area.
Corrective Action: Update the fire district in CAMEO to:
                   "Station 12 - Montpelier Central"

================================================================
REQUIRED ACTIONS AND DEADLINE
================================================================
All four findings must be corrected in CAMEO Data Manager and a
corrected Tier II export must be submitted to the SERC office
by February 15, 2025.
