FINDINGS - GREENFIELD OPERATIONS LLC
================================================================

FINDING G-1: MISSING FACILITY EMERGENCY COORDINATOR
The facility has no designated Facility Emergency Coordinator
on file. The facility's Notes field in CAMEO contains contact
information for a site safety officer who is available 24/7 for
emergency response. Add that individual as a new contact record
with the contact type "Fac. Emergency Coordinator".

FINDING G-2: EHS CLASSIFICATION REVIEW
Review all chemicals stored at Greenfield Operations for
compliance with 40 CFR Part 355 EHS substance designations.
Any chemical stored in average daily quantities exceeding its
Threshold Planning Quantity (TPQ) must be designated as EHS.
Applicable TPQ values:
  - Toluene (CAS 108-88-3):            Not listed (no TPQ)
  - Methanol (CAS 67-56-1):            TPQ = 500 lbs
  - Hydrochloric Acid (CAS 7647-01-0): TPQ = 100 lbs
Verify each chemical's current average daily amount against its
TPQ and correct any non-compliant EHS designations.

FINDING G-3: MISSING FIRE DISTRICT
No fire district is assigned to this facility. Assign:
  "Burlington Fire Station 1 - Downtown"

FINDINGS - RIVERSIDE WATER TREATMENT PLANT
================================================================

FINDING R-1: CHEMICAL QUANTITY ERROR
Hydrogen Peroxide (CAS 7722-84-1) average daily amount is
recorded as 4,500 lbs. The facility's most recent quarterly
inventory audit confirms the correct average is 5,200 lbs.
Update the average daily amount accordingly.

Sulfuric Acid (CAS 7664-93-9) storage location is recorded as
"Outdoor Storage Pad". A January 2025 site inspection confirmed
that all sulfuric acid is now stored in the facility's
"Secondary Containment Building". Update the storage location.

FINDING R-3: OUTDATED EMERGENCY CONTACT PHONE
Emergency Coordinator Maria Santos has a new direct phone
number. Update her contact phone to 802-555-0347.

FINDING R-4: INCORRECT FIRE DISTRICT
The facility record lists "Station 7 - North Montpelier".
The correct responding station based on the facility's address
is "Station 12 - Montpelier Central". Update the fire district.
