STATE EMERGENCY RESPONSE COMMISSION (SERC)
CHEMICAL COMPLIANCE AUDIT NOTICE
Vermont Division of Emergency Management

Date: January 28, 2025
Reference: SERC-2025-VT-CCA-0031

TO: Tier II Reporting Facilities Listed Below
FROM: Vermont SERC Compliance Office
RE: EHS Designation Review - Action Required

BACKGROUND:
The Vermont SERC has completed its annual cross-reference audit of submitted Tier II
reports against the EPA List of Extremely Hazardous Substances (40 CFR Part 355,
Appendix A & B). The audit identified potential discrepancies in EHS substance
designations at the facilities listed below.

Facilities storing any chemical on the EPA EHS List in quantities at or above the
lesser of 500 lbs or the Threshold Planning Quantity (TPQ) MUST designate that
chemical as an EHS in their Tier II submission (EPCRA Section 311/312).

FACILITIES FLAGGED FOR REVIEW:

1. Northfield Paper Mill
   45 Mill Road, Northfield, VT 05663
   NAICS: 322121 (Paper Mills)
   Finding: One or more chemicals used in bleaching/pulp processing operations
   may require EHS designation based on stored quantities relative to applicable
   TPQs. Review all chemicals in your bleaching and chemical treatment processes.

2. Essex Wire and Cable
   211 Industrial Parkway, Essex Junction, VT 05452
   NAICS: 335929 (Wire Manufacturing)
   Finding: One or more chemicals used in manufacturing and refrigeration
   operations may require EHS designation based on stored quantities relative to
   applicable TPQs. Review all chemicals used in your production and refrigeration
   systems.

REQUIRED CORRECTIVE ACTION:
Each flagged facility must:
1. Review the EPA EHS list (https://www.epa.gov/epcra/ehs-list) for all chemicals
   reported in their most recent Tier II filing.
2. Correct EHS designations in their CAMEO Data Manager records for any chemical
   that meets the reporting threshold.
3. Update records no later than February 28, 2025.

Failure to correct EHS designations may result in enforcement action under
EPCRA Section 325.

For questions contact the SERC Compliance Office at (802) 244-8721.

This notice does not constitute an admission of violation.
